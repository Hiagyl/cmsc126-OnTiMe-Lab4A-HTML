Great Pyramid of Giza

The oldest and only surviving wonder of the ancient world, built as a tomb for Pharaoh Khufu.

Colossus of Rhodes

A giant statue of the sun god Helios, symbolizing freedom and victory, destroyed by an earthquake.

Lighthouse of Alexandria

One of the tallest structures of the ancient world, guiding sailors safely to the port of Alexandria.

Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

An elaborate tomb built for Mausolus, renowned for its grand architecture and sculptures.

Temple of Artemis

A grand temple dedicated to the goddess Artemis, known for its intricate designs and massive columns.

Statue of Zeus at Olympia

A magnificent statue of Zeus, seated on a grand throne, once considered the epitome of Greek art.

Hanging Gardens of Babylon

A legendary garden built in the desert, featuring cascading terraces filled with lush greenery.
